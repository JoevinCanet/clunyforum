The Trait d'Union Franco-Allemand Association

The association aims at promoting the French-German citizen-centered approach in the economic and social fields, by facilitating the get-together of citizen and developing a common sense of awareness. Through the organisation of the Cluny Forum, the Trait d'Union Franco-Allemand wants to create a space for debate, as well as critical reflexion of the French-German economic cooperation and integration. The association wants to push the get-together of experts, decision-makers and citizen, to shape together the future of French-German relationships.

The Cluny'16 Organisation Team

Since 2013 and the first edition in Cluny, France, the Trait d'Union Franco-Allemand association organises the Cluny Forum. In order to ensure a successfull forum, the association puts together a motivated and competent team of volunteers. From the organisation of the program, the venue itself or the promotion of the event, the organisation team is in charge of creating a perfect experience for the participants of the Cluny Forum.

Our Projects

During the previous Cluny Forums, the participants put together, on a voluntary basis, a series of projects that target the development of the French-German relationships. Not limited to the duration of the Cluny Forum, these projects are aimed to be continued after the completion of the Forum.
