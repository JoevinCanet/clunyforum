The Projects

Over the course of the last Cluny Forums, along with the participants themselves, the Trait d'Union Franco-Allemand association as been able to develop a series of projects that directly impact the French-German relations and their economic cooperation. In particular the 2015 edition of the Cluny Forum in Berlin made the realisation of these projects possible, which are supposed to continue, even after the duration of the Forum itself. The current three running projects target in particular young French and German people and try to facilitate their access to the job market. We hope the next Cluny Forums will result in other similar projects and further enhance the French-German cooperation.

Title: French-German cross-mentoring program on topics that matter; pilot project with Cluny Forum

Goals: Build a closer Cluny Forum community and develop individual competencies related to French-German topics & development of support and learning competencies, boost communication skills and open mindedness on professional subjects outside the personal comfort zone

Organisation of the program:
- One year duration
- Individual application as a mentor or mentee
- Select Peer-Mentoring groups among e.g. Universities, Grandes Ecoles, etc...
- Scheduled monthly mentoring meetings over a year to follow the mentoring evolution
- A closing workshop and a networking event with all mentor-mentee tandems

Title: How to define and reveal intercultural French-German talent and adapt company talent management programs?

Goals: Enhance existing talent programs by elements related to French-German behaviors, integrate mentors and best practice from an individual company, make companies share mutual experiences and enhance their talents together

Organisation of the program:
- Enhancement of company talent program with elements relevant to new learning techniques and French-German specificities
- Integration of mentors with the relevant experience
- Integration of exchange sessions among talents related to relational issues

Title: Sagarank

Goals:Support unemployed youths to find a training enabling them to get a job in the digital economy

Organisation of the program: Co-construction of a dedicated MOOC with the corporate partner and public job agencies
- Provision of open position by corporate partner to train candidates for
- Awareness building through job agencies and online to attract traffic and users to the platform
- Test and learn of the MOOC with regular feedback
- Follow-up with the corporate partners of first candidates recruited in order to enhance selection and training
